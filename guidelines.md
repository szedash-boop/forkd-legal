# FORKD — Community Guidelines

**Effective date:** 2026-05-07
**Version:** 2026-05-06

FORKD is a place to share what you eat with people who care. Keep it generous, keep it honest, and treat other users the way you would treat someone sharing a table with you.

These Guidelines work alongside our [Terms of Service](./terms). Violating them can result in content removal, feature limits, or account termination.

---

## What's welcome

- Photos of food you've actually eaten.
- Honest reviews — positive or critical — written in your own voice.
- Recipes, lists, recommendations, restaurant tips.
- Friendly disagreement and curiosity.
- Reporting things that break these rules.

## What's not allowed

### 1. Harassment, hate, and threats

- No targeted harassment, bullying, or pile-ons.
- No slurs, hate speech, or content that attacks people based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic.
- No threats of violence, doxxing, or sharing private information about another person.

### 2. Sexual content and nudity

- No pornographic content, sexually explicit imagery, or sexualization of minors.
- No nudity in photos or profile media.

### 3. Illegal content and dangerous activity

- No content that promotes, glorifies, or facilitates illegal drugs, weapons, fraud, theft, or violence.
- No content depicting or encouraging self-harm or eating disorders.

### 4. Misinformation and impersonation

- No impersonation of other people, restaurants, brands, or FORKD staff.
- No fabricated reviews — do not post about places you have not been or food you have not eaten.
- No paid or sponsored content disguised as an organic review. Disclosure is required.

### 5. Spam, scams, and manipulation

- No bulk posting, repetitive content, or follow-for-follow schemes.
- No phishing, scams, fake giveaways, or malicious links.
- No vote, like, or follow manipulation.
- No mass-reporting users you disagree with — fraudulent reports are themselves a violation.

### 6. Privacy

- No sharing of other people's personal information (phone numbers, addresses, email, ID documents) without consent.
- No photographs of other diners taken without their consent in private settings.

### 7. Restaurants and businesses

- Reviews must be your honest first-hand experience.
- If you have a financial or personal relationship with a place (you own it, work there, or were paid to post), disclose it clearly.
- Do not use FORKD to extort restaurants ("post nice or I will leave a 1-star review").

## How we enforce these rules

- **Automated checks** run on posts before they appear publicly.
- **Reports from users** route to a moderation queue. Anyone can report a post, comment, or profile from the "..." menu.
- **Human review** is performed in a timely manner. We may remove content, restrict features, or suspend or terminate accounts.
- **Appeals**: if you believe an enforcement action was made in error, reply to the email notice or write to **szedash@gmail.com**.

## Reporting and blocking

- **Report content:** tap "..." on any post, comment, or profile and choose **Report**.
- **Block a user:** tap "..." on their profile and choose **Block**. Blocked users cannot see your posts, comment on your posts, or message you.
- **Emergency:** if you believe someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services first, then email us at **szedash@gmail.com**.

## Children and minors

FORKD is rated **12+** and is not intended for users under **13**. Do not post identifiable photos, full names, or location details of minors who have not consented (or whose guardians have not consented).

## Content beyond the app's age rating

FORKD does not host creator content above its 12+ rating. If you encounter such content, report it. Repeated violations result in account termination.

## Contact

Questions, appeals, or safety concerns: **szedash@gmail.com**.
